Video Service
=============
.. note::
  The Video Service is available in SDK versions 2.1.0 and later. Prior to 2.1.0 the methods were part of the :cpp:func:`Conference Service <dolbyio::comms::services::conference::start_video>`. 

The Video Service allows managing the local participant's video stream.
